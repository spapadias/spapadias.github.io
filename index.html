I am a Research Associate and a PhD Candidate of Computer Science at the Technical University of Berlin (TUB), working with Prof. Volker Markl at the Database Systems and Information Management (DIMA) group.

I received my Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in Computer Science from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2018, where I worked on worst-case optimal stream join algorithms with Prof. Ke Yi, who has a beautiful mind.

In September 2015, I received my 5-year Diploma M. Eng. Degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from National Technical University of Athens (NTUA), where I worked my diploma thesis under the under the guidance of Kostas Patroumpas, who I rather consider as a "(scientific) father" than an advisor.
